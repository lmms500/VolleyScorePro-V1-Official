Privacy Policy

VolleyScore Pro - Last updated: February 2026

Thank you for using VolleyScore Pro. This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our application.

1. Information We Collect

VolleyScore Pro collects the following information when you use the application:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and profile picture (when you sign in with your Google account)
  • Match Data: Match history, player statistics, and game settings you create
  • Images: Profile photos and team logos you upload (stored securely)
  • Usage Data: Anonymous information about how you use the app to improve the experience

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the application services
  • Sync your data across devices (when you are signed in)
  • Save your match history to the cloud
  • Personalize your in-app experience
  • Send relevant notifications about your matches
  • Improve our services based on anonymous usage

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information.

We share information only with:

  • Google Firebase: For authentication, data storage, and hosting
  • Google AdMob: To display advertisements (anonymous data only)
  • You: Your data is always available for export

4. Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your information:

  • Data encryption in transit (HTTPS)
  • Data encryption at rest (Firebase)
  • Secure authentication via Google OAuth
  • Restricted database access rules

5. Your Rights (LGPD/GDPR)

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Rectify: Update incorrect information
  • Delete: Request deletion of your data
  • Portability: Export your data in a readable format
  • Revoke Consent: Disconnect your Google account at any time

6. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active or as necessary to provide services. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time.

7. Children

VolleyScore Pro is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from minors. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information, please contact us.

8. Cookies and Similar Technologies

The application may use technologies such as:

  • LocalStorage: To save application preferences
  • IndexedDB: For offline functionality
  • Advertising identifiers: For ad personalization (can be disabled in device settings)

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or by email. Continued use of the application after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
